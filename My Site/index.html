About me


Kim Morgan is a United States Navy Veteran and Claims Specialist. She received her Master’s Degree in Organizational Leadership from Brandman University in 2015. She has worked for the Social Security Administration as a Claims Specialist from 2016 to 2021 and is currently a student of the San Diego Global Knowledge University’s Full Stack Development Immersive Program. Kim currently resides in Tolleson, Arizona and enjoys being in nature, spending time with family and volunteering in the local community.
